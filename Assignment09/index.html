Chocolate Dipped Ghost Pretzels

What You'll Need

  • 1 Bag of White candy melts
  • 1 tsp of Coconut Oil
  • Bag of Rold Gold Pretzel Rods
  • Black Editible Marker
  • Baking sheet (optional)
  • Enough Parchment Paper to cover Baking sheet/work area

Step 1 - Prepair the Chocolate

Microwave white candy melts and 1 tsp coconut oil for 20-sec. In increments, stir until melted and smooth.

Step 2 - Take Out Pretzel Rods

Then, dip ends of pretzel rods two-thirds of the way in candy melts, shaking off excess, and place on the parchment.

Step 3 - Dry and Decorate

Once completely dry, use black edible marker to create your ghost faces.
